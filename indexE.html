CUSTOMER RELATIONSHIP

At the moment we have not dealt with customers, but in the future we plan to give away small samples of our perfume at the science fair.

CHANNELS

Our distribution channel is direct, that is, it goes from the producer to the final consumer without the intervention of the wholesaler and a retailer.

CUSTOMER SEGMENTS

In many cases people tend to have a bad smell which can be quite annoying, some people have this problem more present than others and our product helps to avoid that problem.

WHO ARE WE?

We are GAMA PERFUME, we will make a perfume that eliminates bad odors and without the use of chemicals that can cause damage in the future.

KEY PARTNERS.

At the moment we are only a group of 4 partners willing to expand the business and open up to the ideas of new partners.

KEY ACTIVITIES

We have carried out different activities to carry out the project, including the chemical part involved in the elaboration of the perfume and the research part involved in the written work.

KEY RESOURCES.

Among the resources we have used are materials for manufacturing such as essences, water and alcohol, as well as the computers on which we work for activities that require these.

VALUE PROPOSITIONS.

Our perfume, as it does not use chemicals, is friendlier to the environment and your skin, as well as being a cheaper option.
